Arikesari Maravarman
Arikesari Maravarman , also known as Parankusa, was a Pandya king of early medieval south India.

Arikesari's reign witnessed the beginning of the Pandya contest with the Pallavas in the northern Tamil country.

He probably entered into an alliance with Chalukyas (to the counter the Pallavas).

The Pandyas also came into collision with the Cheras under his rule.
