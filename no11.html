Maravarman Rajasimha
Maravarman Rajasimha I , also known as Pallavabhanjana, was a Pandya king of early medieval south India.

He was the son and successor of Ko Chadaiyan Ranadhira.

He remembered for his important successes against the Pallavas and in the Kongu country.

Although mentioned in the Larger Sinnamanur Plates, the Velvikkudi Grant is the major source of information about this Pandya king
