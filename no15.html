Parantaka Nedunchadaiyan
Srimara was born to Pandya king Parantaka Nedunchadaiyan (Varaguna I).

The Larger Sinnamanur Plates tells that Srimara defeated the "Mayapandya", the Kerala (Chera), the king of Simhala, the Pallava and the Vallabha.

The Tamil potion claims victories at Kunnur and Vizhinjam as well as in Sri Lanka.

It also describes his repulsion of an alliance of the Gangas, the Pallavas, the Cholas and others at Kudamukku (Kumbakonam).
