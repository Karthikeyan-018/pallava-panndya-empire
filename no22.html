Battle at Kumbakonam
Srimara was defeated at Tellaru (Wandiwash/Vanthavachi, North Arcot) by an alliance lead by Pallava ruler Nandivarman III.

The Pallava allies were the Gangas, the Cholas and the Rashtrakutas.

The Pandyas retreated southwards into their home country (and the Pallava army even advanced as far the Vaigai).

However, Srimara was able defeat the Pallava alliance in a battle near Kumabakonam

Srimarara was then defeated by the Pallava king Nripatunga in the battle of Arichil.
